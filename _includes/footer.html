csv,conf,v3 is about bringing a diverse group together to discuss data topics. There will be a limited number of travel awards available for those unable to pay. We are a community organized not-for-profit event.

We strive to be a supportive and welcoming environment to all attendees. We encourage you to read the Conf Code of Conduct and will be enforcing it.

CSV Conf is a group effort to put on a non-profit, community conference with dozens of people putting in time, including but not limited to the organizing team:

  • John Chodacki, California Digital Library
  • Max Ogden, Dat
  • Martin Fenner, DataCite
